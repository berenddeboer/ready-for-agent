# Risk-Based Local Review Reruns

Reviewing passes report one aggregate Review Severity using an explicit impact rubric: low, medium, or high. Changed work from medium or high findings always runs Pre-Commit and another full reviewing pass; changed work from low findings runs Pre-Commit and then a short build-model Review Rerun Assessment, which may produce an Accepted Review Outcome only for direct, localized, semantics-preserving remediation and otherwise requires full review. Low and medium findings may be deferred, changed-and-deferred is represented explicitly, and low or medium findings rejected without changes produce a distinct Cleared Review Outcome; unresolved or disputed high findings require human attention.

The assessment returns a binary decision with a rationale, falls back to full review on malformed or uncertain output, and trusts the shared Session's account of apply and Pre-Commit changes rather than capturing an exact repository delta. This deliberately accepts that silent hook rewrites may be missed in exchange for less plumbing; the final outcome summary is stored on the Step Run, per-round state remains in memory, and Retry starts a fresh full review.
